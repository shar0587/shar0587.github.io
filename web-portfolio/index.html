About Myself

That's a tough one...I like a bunch of stuff!

But to summarize, I'm a content creator based in Ottawa (the capital city of Canada) specializing in editing, and creating content for myself and others. I'm also an aspiring photographer, videographer, model, and fitness coach.

Growing up, I've always known I wanted to make a name for myself in some way. To continuously become the best version of myself that I can be. When I caught the creative bug during my school career, I knew this was the space to be in. To express yourself in many ways in all shapes and sizes in the digital world. That's what content creation means to me: the art of expression.

In my free time, you can find me playing Warzone with my friends, working out, chilling with my dog and family, eating good food, and just having a good time in general. Life is too short to take things so seriously!
